Our Story

From a tiny corner café to a beloved community space — the Brew & Bloom journey.

Est. 2012

More than coffee —
it's a community

It all began in 2012 when founders Maria and James Chen noticed something missing in their Brooklyn neighborhood: a place to slow down. Not a grab-and-go counter, but a real destination — warm, welcoming, and worth lingering in.

They started small: one espresso machine, six tables, and a rotating selection of single-origin beans sourced directly from farmers they'd personally met on trips to Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala.

Word spread fast. Today Brew & Bloom serves over 500 guests daily, yet the spirit remains unchanged: every cup made with care, every guest made to feel at home.
